Product Requirements Document (PRD) 

1. Overview 

The objective of this product is to build a simple, mobile-first business accounting application for small and medium businesses (retailers, wholesalers, shopkeepers) to manage cash, stock, sales, expenses, staff, customers, suppliers, and banks in one place. 

The app should be easy to use, Urdu + English supported, and inspired by successful solutions like DigiKhata, while covering the client‚Äôs specific requirements.
